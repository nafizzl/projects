AMBULANE

Ambulane is an app my 4-person team submitted at my first hackathon, Axxess Hackathon 2023, held at the University of Texas at Dallas. It was my first exposure to creating something outside of classwork activities. The idea involves allowing first responders to operate like an "official" Waze, sending OS notifications to people along the path an EMT is taking.

Frontend: Figma, HTML, CSS

Backend: Python, Bun

Authentication/Notification: AWS Lambda

JAKEBOT

JakeBot is an app my 4-person team submitted at my second hackathon, HackUTD X, held at the University of Texas at Dallas. My team created an improved chatbot for State Farm, one which would take care of simple tasks for clients like getting quotes and analyzing official insurance documentation, which would be especially helpful for people like small business owners.

Frontend: HTML, TailwindCSS, Vue, Kommunicate API

Backend: Gooogle Firestore

Chatbot Mapping: DialogFlow CX

Cloud Deployment: Google Firebase
